About us

Jeanbaptistegroup is a Stockholm-based production company specializing in live production, choreography, creative direction and visual concepts.

Founded by creative director and choreographer Sascha Jean-Baptiste, the company has built a global reputation for creating visually iconic performances and large-scale productions for some of the world's biggest artists, television productions and live entertainment platforms.
